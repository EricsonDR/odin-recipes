The Best Burger You'll Ever Eat!

The cheeseburger recipe is not only easy to follow, but it is the tastiest cheeseburger you'll ever eat. Spend some time going through the recipe and wow guests at your next get-together!

Ingredients

  • 1lb. Hamburger meat (80% lean / 20% fat)
  • Kinder "Buttery Steakhouse" seasoning
  • 1 Artisan hamburger bun with seasame seeds on top
  • 2 Slices of Tillamook pre-sliced sharp cheddar cheese.
  • Inglehoffer stone ground mustard
  • G. Hughes No Sugar Added BBQ sauce

Directions

  1. Pre-heat your grill to 300 degrees
  2. Split the 1lb. of hamburger meat in to two, mostly equal sized peices.
  3. Form a round ball of hamburger with each peice and then slowly press it into the shape of a hamburger patty.
  4. Liberally apply Kinder "Buttery Steakhouse" seasoning to both sides of both patties and push the seasoning in as much as possible.
  5. Now grill the patties on the preheated grill to your desired finished temperature.
  6. Add cheese as grilling nears completion so it is melted before building your burger.
  7. Split apart the halves of the hamburger bun and put them "inside-down" on the grill as it nears completion.
  8. When finished, take the bottom part of the bun off first and add it to your plate.
  9. Next, add the first hamburger with cheese and slather Inglehoffer mustard on it.
  10. Take the second burger and place it on top. Slather G. Hughes BBQ sauge on it.
  11. Finally add the top of the bun.
  12. Enjoy!

Note: feel free to add condiments as desired to get the perfect flavor you are looking for.
